Overview

The Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) is a proven, publicly available framework for applying Lean-Agile practices at enterprise scale. SAFe helps large organizations address the significant challenges of developing and delivering high-quality software and systems in the shortest sustainable lead time. It provides a knowledge base of integrated, proven patterns for enterprise Lean-Agile development. Companies can leverage SAFe to improve time-to-market, increase productivity, and enhance employee engagement.

Why Choose SAFe?

Companies should use SAFe because:

  • It scales Agile practices to the enterprise level, enabling alignment, collaboration, and delivery across multiple teams.
  • It provides a structured approach to Lean-Agile transformation, with guidance on roles, responsibilities, and best practices.
  • It fosters innovation and continuous improvement by emphasizing feedback loops and decentralized decision-making.
  • It enhances organizational agility, allowing companies to adapt quickly to changing market conditions and customer needs.
